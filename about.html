About Beim Health

AI that turns your
health benefits into action.

Beim Health is an AI-driven platform that synthesizes clinical intelligence, product data, personal health goals, and your financial position — HSA, FSA, or out-of-pocket — to help you make smarter, faster health decisions.

🧬
Clinical Intelligence
Evidence-graded recommendations backed by peer-reviewed research and physician confidence scores.
📊
Product Intelligence
Every product scored A–D on our Clinical Evidence Level rubric. Science, not marketing.
🎯
Goals & Context
Your Care Navigator adapts to your health goals, conditions, and lifestyle — not a one-size-fits-all catalog.
💳
Financial Position
HSA, FSA, and OOP balances — unified. We optimize spend, handle LMNs, and prevent forfeiture.
Our Mission

Building the decision engine for
consumer health commerce.

Beim Health combines clinical AI, objective product data, and real-time financial context to give consumers a single source of truth for every health purchase. We ensure people access provider-vetted solutions matched to their goals and budget — while giving physicians a scalable way to recommend quality products backed by evidence, not ad spend.

We are eliminating the information asymmetry that has plagued healthcare commerce. Every product is clinically graded, every dollar tracked, every Letter of Medical Necessity handled — so your benefits work for you instead of expiring unused.
Clinical intelligence meets financial intelligence — no middlemen, just better decisions.
— The principle behind everything we build

Our Story

From clinical rooms to
an AI decision engine.

Beim started with a simple observation: people were leaving money on the table because no one connected what they needed, what was eligible, and what actually worked.

🔍
The Insight
25 years watching healthcare fail consumers
Our founding team spent decades in clinical operations, radiology AI, population health, and enterprise finance — watching firsthand as administrative friction, opaque pricing, and information asymmetry drained value from patients and providers alike.
◆ Combined 25+ yrs clinical & executive experience
🧪
The MVP
502 Health: validating demand
We tested our thesis through an e-commerce pilot — 5,598 unique visitors, a $183 average cart value, and a clear signal: consumers want clinically graded, HSA-eligible products. They just need a system smart enough to connect the dots.
◆ 5,598 visitors · $183 avg cart
⚡
The Pivot
From storefront to AI platform
The biggest unlock wasn't a better catalog — it was intelligence. $150B in HSA/FSA capital sitting behind paperwork no one completes. So we built a platform: AI Care Navigators, instant LMN generation, clinical product grading, and financial optimization — all direct-to-consumer.
◆ $150B addressable locked capital
🚀
Today
Hundreds of products. Four intelligence layers. Zero guesswork.
Beim Health is live with a growing catalog of CEL-graded products, AI Care Navigators (Everly & Brooks), HSA/FSA split checkout, Community Pool group buying, and a HIPAA-compliant precision portal — all powered by clinical, product, goal, and financial intelligence working together.
◆ Live & scaling · 4 AI intelligence layers

What We Believe

Principles that drive every decision.

🔬
Evidence Over Marketing
Every product is graded A–D on our Clinical Evidence Level rubric. You see the science — RCTs, physician confidence scores, and clinical criteria — not ad copy.
💡
Radical Transparency
No hidden fees, no affiliate-driven recommendations. We show what your HSA covers, what you'll pay out of pocket, and why a product was recommended — every time.
🤝
Access as a Right
Tax-advantaged funds exist so people can afford better care. The paperwork wall that blocks 85% of consumers from using those funds is a problem worth solving.
🛡️
Privacy First
HIPAA-compliant by design. We don't sell your data, serve ads, or share your health information. Your data stays encrypted and under your control.
🧠
AI That Serves You
Our Care Navigators optimize for your goals and budget, not our revenue. Intelligence that simplifies decisions — not upsells.
⚖️
Free Market Healthcare
We remove middlemen, not add them. Direct provider access, transparent pricing, and informed consumers make healthcare work the way it should.
