Questions

Common practice questions.

How does the EMR integration actually work?▼
Beim connects to your EMR (NextGen, eClinicalWorks, Epic, athenahealth, and FHIR-compatible systems) via a secure API gateway. Once authorized, we can cross-reference patient biometrics, ICD-10 codes, and lab results with our product catalog to generate clinically relevant recommendations. The integration is read-only — Beim never writes to your medical records. Setup typically takes one session with your IT contact.
How does the revenue share work?▼
When a patient purchases a product through Beim that was recommended via your practice's integration, you receive a percentage of the sale. The exact revenue share depends on your partnership tier — Design Partners (early adopters) receive a preferred rate locked in for the life of the partnership. There are no fees, no minimums, and no clawbacks. Revenue is paid monthly.
Do I need to stock inventory or manage fulfillment?▼
No. Beim handles all fulfillment directly-to-consumer. Products ship from our brand partners to the patient's door. Your practice never touches inventory, manages shipping, or handles returns. You simply review and sign off on LMNs when flagged — everything else is automated.
What is the Clinical Evidence Level (CEL) grading?▼
Every product in Beim's catalog is graded A through D using our Clinical Evidence Level rubric. Grade A means multi-RCT evidence with 90–100% physician confidence. Grade D means early-stage or limited evidence. This grading system lets you and your patients make informed decisions, and it protects your clinical reputation — you're never putting your name on unvetted products.
How does the one-click LMN sign-off work?▼
When a patient's clinical data suggests a product that requires a Letter of Medical Necessity for HSA/FSA eligibility, the system flags it for your review. You see the patient context, the product, and the eligibility rationale in a single view. One click generates a signed digital LMN — instantly unlocking the patient's tax-advantaged funds. The typical physician time per LMN is under 30 seconds.
Is patient data shared with anyone?▼
Beim is HIPAA-compliant. Patient health data is used solely to generate product recommendations and is never shared with third parties, advertisers, or employers. The EMR integration is read-only — we analyze data in place and only surface recommendations. Patients can also use Beim without the EMR integration by self-reporting their health goals directly.
What does "Design Partner" mean?▼
Design Partners are the first practices in each market to adopt Beim. In exchange for early feedback on the EMR workflow and product catalog, Design Partners receive a preferred revenue share that's locked in for the life of the partnership, priority feature requests, and first access to new integrations. It's our way of rewarding practices that help us build the best possible clinical workflow.
What kind of products are in the catalog?▼
Beim's catalog spans thousands of HSA/FSA-eligible products across 25+ categories — including lab tests, telehealth programs, weight management, GLP-1 programs, hair loss treatments, skincare, sleep aids, supplements, digital health devices, and more. All products are clinically graded, and you can control which categories are recommended to your patients.
