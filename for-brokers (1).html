Questions

Common broker questions.

How is this actually free?▼
Beim earns a margin on products purchased through the platform — similar to how a marketplace works. We don't charge brokers, employers, or employees any fees. Your clients get a free benefit enhancement; we get customers who buy health products.
Does the employer need to do anything to set this up?▼
Nothing. No IT integration, no API setup, no HR admin. Employees self-serve — they visit beim.ai, enter their HSA/FSA balance, and start shopping. The employer just needs to share the link (we provide email templates and open enrollment materials).
Is employee health data shared with the broker or employer?▼
Absolutely not. Beim is HIPAA-compliant. Employee health data, purchase history, and biometrics are never shared with brokers, employers, or any third party. Employees control their own data.
What does "co-branded" mean?▼
When you sign up as a broker partner, we create a landing page that includes your firm's name — so when employees visit, they see that this benefit was brought to them through your brokerage. It reinforces your value without any additional work.
Can I use Beim during open enrollment?▼
That's the ideal time. During OE, employees are already thinking about benefits. Include Beim in your enrollment materials as "a free HSA/FSA optimization tool" — it gives employees an immediate reason to fund their accounts and choose higher-deductible plans.
How does Beim handle Letters of Medical Necessity?▼
Three pathways at checkout: (1) Use Beim's network physician — $35, signed in ~4 hours; (2) We generate a pre-filled template the employee takes to their own doctor; (3) Employee uploads an LMN they already have. The whole process is integrated into checkout.
