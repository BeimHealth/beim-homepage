Questions

Common partner questions.

What does it cost to list my product?▼
Nothing. There are no listing fees, setup fees, or monthly charges. Beim earns a margin on products sold through the platform — that's it. You set your wholesale price, we handle the rest. You only pay when you make a sale.
How does the Clinical Evidence Level grading work?▼
Our clinical team reviews published evidence for every product and assigns a CEL grade: A (multi-RCT, 90–100% physician confidence), B (single RCT or strong observational data), C (emerging evidence or expert consensus), or D (limited evidence). Grade A products receive premium visibility in AI matching. This rubric builds trust with consumers, physicians, and employers — making your product easier to sell.
How do I handle fulfillment?▼
You ship direct-to-consumer. When a buyer purchases your product on Beim, you receive the order details and ship from your existing fulfillment infrastructure. Beim handles payment processing, HSA/FSA compliance, LMN facilitation, and customer communication. You handle the product and shipping — exactly as you would with any D2C channel.
Is my product HSA/FSA-eligible?▼
Many health products are HSA/FSA-eligible either by default (lab tests, medical devices, prescribed treatments) or with a Letter of Medical Necessity. If your product requires an LMN, Beim handles the entire process — $35 via our physician network, pre-filled templates, or patient upload. This is the #1 barrier to HSA/FSA purchases, and we've automated it.
How does the AI match my product to buyers?▼
When consumers enter Beim, they set health goals (weight, sleep, hair, stress, energy, etc.), enter their HSA/FSA balance, and share insurance details. Our AI Care Navigators — Everly and Brooks — match them with products ranked by clinical grade, relevance to their goals, price fit, and eligibility. Your product surfaces when it's the right match — not when you pay for placement.
What distribution channels will my product reach?▼
Four channels: (1) Direct consumers via beim.ai and the AI navigator; (2) Employer benefits programs that distribute Beim to their workforce; (3) Benefits brokers who offer Beim to their employer clients; (4) Physician practices with EMR integration who can recommend your product clinically. One listing reaches all four simultaneously.
How long does the application process take?▼
We review every application within 48 hours. Clinical grading typically takes 3–5 business days depending on the evidence base. Most products are live on the marketplace within one week of application. For products with strong clinical evidence (Grade A or B), we can often expedite the process.
