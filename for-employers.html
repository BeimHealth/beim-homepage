Questions

Common employer questions.

How is this actually free for my company?▼
Beim earns a margin on products purchased through the platform — similar to how any marketplace works. We don't charge employers or employees any fees. You get a free benefit enhancement; we get customers who buy health products. Our incentives are aligned: the more employees use Beim, the better it is for everyone.
Does my IT team need to do anything?▼
Nothing at all. No API integration, no SSO setup, no data feeds. Employees self-serve — they visit beim.ai, enter their HSA/FSA balance, and start shopping. Your only job is sharing the link (we provide email templates and open enrollment materials ready to go).
Is employee health data shared with us?▼
Absolutely not. Beim is HIPAA-compliant by design. Employee health data, purchase history, biometrics, and Care Navigator conversations are never shared with employers, HR, or any third party. Employees control their own data completely.
What products can employees buy?▼
Beim offers 187+ HSA/FSA-eligible products across 25 categories — including lab tests, telehealth programs, supplements, skincare, sleep aids, weight management, hair loss treatments, and more. Every product is graded A–D using our Clinical Evidence Level (CEL) rubric, so employees can see the science behind what they're buying.
How does Beim handle Letters of Medical Necessity?▼
Three pathways at checkout: (1) Use Beim's network physician — $35, signed in ~4 hours; (2) We generate a pre-filled template the employee takes to their own doctor; (3) Employee uploads an LMN they already have. The whole process is integrated into checkout — no separate steps, no waiting weeks.
When is the best time to roll this out?▼
Open enrollment is ideal — employees are already thinking about benefits, so introducing Beim as a free HSA/FSA tool drives immediate engagement. But Beim works year-round. You can share the link anytime via email, Slack, your benefits portal, or a company newsletter. There's no wrong time to help employees stop forfeiting money.
What if we already have a wellness platform?▼
Beim isn't a wellness platform — it's an HSA/FSA purchasing and navigation tool. It complements existing wellness programs by giving employees a way to buy clinically graded products with pre-tax dollars they're already sitting on. Think of it as the spending side of the equation, not the engagement side.
